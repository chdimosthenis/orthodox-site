In English translations of the [Bible](/Bible "Bible"), the word heavens can mean sky, or world above earth. For example, in [Genesis](/Genesis "Genesis") 1:1, '[God](/God "God") created the heavens and the earth.' However, the word in the singular is used to describe the place where God and [angels](/Angel "Angel") dwell- cf Genesis 22:15, 'the Lord's angel called out from heaven'.

Some of the most vivid descriptions of heaven are given in the [Book of Revelation](/Book_of_Revelation "Book of Revelation").
